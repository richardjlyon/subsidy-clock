Put the Clock on your site. It updates itself.

One line of HTML embeds the live counter. The figure refreshes from the Clock's published data, so an embed placed today is correct forever. It sets no cookies and makes no requests from your page beyond the iframe itself. Attribution and the as-of date are part of the widget.

<iframe src="https://subsidyclock.co.uk/embed/widget?size=banner" width="728" height="90" title="The Subsidy Clock" sandbox="allow-scripts allow-popups allow-top-navigation-by-user-activation" loading="lazy" style="border:0"></iframe>

Add &theme=dark to the URL for the dark variant. If JavaScript is blocked, the widget shows the figure stamped at the most recent daily build, with its date.
