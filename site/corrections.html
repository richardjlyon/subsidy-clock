Corrections

This site reconstructs official data, and reconstruction can contain errors. If you find one, I want to know. Confirmed errors are corrected and logged below, with credit to the finder if they wish.

Reporting an error

Report an error — the email opens with the page's details attached; you supply only what you believe is wrong and, if you wish, your evidence and a name for credit. Every figure on the dashboard also carries its own report mark, which attaches that figure's details automatically.

Three things can be true of a disputed number. If the figure is wrong, it is fixed and logged here. If the figure is right under the stated method and the disagreement is with the method, that is answered on the methodology page — a definitional dispute is not an error. And if a source has revised its own history, that is logged automatically in the restatements file.
